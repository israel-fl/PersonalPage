Your privacy is important to Israel Fl. This privacy statement provides information about the personal information that Israel Fl collects, and the ways in which Israel Fl uses that personal information.

Using personal information

Israel Fl may use your personal information to:

Israel Fl will take reasonable technical and organisational precautions to prevent the loss, misuse or alteration of your personal information.
